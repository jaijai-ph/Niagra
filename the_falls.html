THE FALLS
Map
With a vertical drop spanning more than 165 feet, Niagara Falls has the highest flow rate of any waterfall in world. The natural phenomenon known as Niagara Falls is the collective term for three waterfalls, located on the Niagara River. Lying between United States and Canada, the American Falls, Horseshoe Falls and the Bridal Veil Falls together form Niagara Falls.
